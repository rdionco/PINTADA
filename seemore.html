ABOUT US

Pintada is a cultural platform dedicated to preserving, promoting, and showcasing Philippine art, heritage, and identity through meaningful digital experiences.

Vision

To become a trusted space where Philippine culture and creativity are celebrated, preserved, and shared with future generations.

Mission

To highlight Filipino artistry, support cultural education, and create an accessible platform that connects people to the nation’s artistic heritage.
