PINTADA

Bombing of the Intendencia

Artist: Fernando Cueto Amorsolo

Year: 1942

Location: Jorge B. Vargas Museum, University of the Philippines, Diliman

"Bombing of the Intendencia" is a painting by Fernando Amorsolo during the Japanese occupation. It shows the Intendencia building of Intramuros engulfed in flames after being bombed. Amorsolo used a dramatic color contrast of dark smoke and bright yellow and orange flames to emphasize the destruction. It also shows the panic and suffering of civilians as they fled the area with their belongings, capturing the emotional effects of the bombing.
